About the Project

Demonstrating a real-time monitoring system for Semcon’s coffee maker highlights the transformative potential of Industry 4.0 technologies, offering our customers an engaging glimpse into smarter and connected solutions.

By using sensors to monitor the coffee maker’s performance data, we can analyze this information to identify potential issues and optimize its operation for quality, efficiency and sustainability (no waste). The results are then visualized using a dashboard, providing a clear and intuitive way to monitor the coffee maker in real-time.
